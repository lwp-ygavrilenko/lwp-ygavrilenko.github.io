Privacy Policy

General

MusicIntervals2Anki is developed by a group of developers.

Privacy Policy describes how information obtained from users is collected, used and disclosed.

By using MusicIntervals2Anki, you agree that your personal information will be handled as described in this Privacy Policy.

Information being collected

MusicIntervals2Anki does not collect any personal identifiable information.

The `WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` permission is used for selecting video files to be converted to audio, writing captured audio to files, selecting audio files to be added to AnkiDroid.

The `RECORD_AUDIO` permission is used for capturing media sounds from other applications to then be saved into files and added to AnkiDroid.
